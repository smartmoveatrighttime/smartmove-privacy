Privacy Policy for SmartMove

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Introduction

SmartMove ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SmartMove mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using SmartMove, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Summary

  • What we collect: Account info, location, payment details, usage data
  • Why we collect it: To provide delivery services, improve the app, ensure security
  • Who we share with: Drivers (for delivery), service providers (Stripe, Firebase, Google Maps)
  • Your rights: Access, update, delete your data; control location and notifications
  • Contact: privacy@smartmove.co

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
  • Delivery Addresses: Pickup and delivery locations you provide

1.2 Location Information

  • Real-time Location: GPS location when you book a delivery or track an order
  • Saved Addresses: Locations you save for quick booking

We use location data to:

  • Calculate delivery pricing
  • Match you with drivers
  • Provide real-time tracking
  • Display nearby pickup/delivery locations

1.3 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • IP address

1.4 Usage Information

  • Orders placed and delivery history
  • App interactions and features used
  • Loyalty points and rewards earned
  • Subscription status (Premium/Corporate)
  • Chat messages with drivers
  • App crashes and performance data

1.5 Payment Information

  • Payment method details (securely processed by Stripe)
  • Transaction history
  • In-app purchase receipts (processed by Apple/Google)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing and fulfilling delivery orders
  • Matching you with available drivers
  • Providing real-time tracking
  • Processing payments securely
  • Sending order updates and notifications

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Developing new features
  • Optimizing user experience

2.3 Communication

  • Sending order confirmations and updates
  • Notifying you about your deliveries
  • Responding to customer support requests
  • Sending promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Communicating account or policy changes

2.4 Loyalty & Rewards

  • Tracking points and tier status
  • Managing subscriptions (Premium/Corporate)
  • Administering scratch cards and rewards
  • Processing referral bonuses

2.5 Safety & Security

  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Ensuring platform security
  • Resolving disputes
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

3.1 Drivers

  • Your name and contact information
  • Pickup and delivery addresses
  • Order details
  • In-app messages

3.2 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Firebase: Backend services and authentication
  • Google Maps: Location services and mapping
  • Cloud messaging: Push notifications
  • Analytics providers: App performance monitoring

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • PCI-compliant payment processing (Stripe)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

  • View and update your profile information in the App
  • Manage saved addresses
  • Review order history

5.2 Location Services

  • Enable/disable location access in device settings
  • Note: Location is required for core delivery functionality

5.3 Notifications

  • Manage push notification preferences in App settings
  • Opt out of promotional communications (delivery updates will continue)

5.4 Account Deletion

  • Request account deletion via App settings or contact us
  • We will delete your personal information within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance

5.5 Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • We will provide data in a commonly used format

6. Children's Privacy

SmartMove is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Region-Specific Information

For Users in the European Union (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent

For Users in California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information

We do not sell personal information.

For Users in Singapore (PDPA)

We comply with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012. You may:

  • Access and correct your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request information about how your data is used


SmartMove is committed to protecting your privacy.
