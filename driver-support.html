Getting Started

How do I sign up as a driver?

Download Smart Move Driver from the App Store or Google Play. Register with your phone number, complete your profile, upload required documents (ID, driver's license, vehicle registration), and wait for verification approval (usually 1-2 business days).

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid driver's license, vehicle registration/insurance, a clear profile photo, and a photo of your vehicle. Additional documents may be required based on your region.

How long does verification take?

Document verification typically takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive a notification once approved. If additional information is needed, we'll contact you via the app.

Jobs & Deliveries

How do I receive job offers?

Make sure you're online in the app and location services are enabled. Jobs will appear based on your proximity to pickup locations. You can accept or decline offers based on your availability.

Can I cancel an accepted job?

You can cancel before pickup, but frequent cancellations may affect your acceptance rate and tier status. If there's an emergency, contact support immediately.

What if the customer is not available?

Wait for the designated time shown in the app. Try calling the customer through the in-app call feature. If unreachable, follow the prompts to mark as "Customer Unavailable" and follow return instructions.

How do I handle fragile items?

Items marked as "FRAGILE" require extra care. Use appropriate handling during pickup and delivery. Take photos of the package condition at pickup for your protection.

Earnings & Payments

How are my earnings calculated?

Earnings are based on base fare + distance + any applicable bonuses. Your driver tier affects your earnings multiplier. View the breakdown for each completed job in your Earnings tab.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Go to Earnings ‚Üí Withdraw. Enter the amount and confirm. Withdrawals are processed via Stripe to your registered bank account. Processing typically takes 1-3 business days.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum withdrawal amount is $10. There are no withdrawal fees charged by Smart Move.

How do I update my bank details?

Go to Profile ‚Üí Bank Account. You can update your bank details at any time. Changes may require re-verification for security purposes.

Technical Issues

The app is running slowly

Try: 1) Close and restart the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Update to the latest version, 4) Restart your device, 5) Clear app cache in device settings.

I'm not receiving job notifications

Check: 1) Notifications are enabled in device settings, 2) You're set to "Online" in the app, 3) Location services are enabled, 4) Battery optimization isn't blocking the app.

GPS/Location not working properly

Ensure location is set to "Always Allow" for Smart Move Driver. Try toggling location services off and on. Make sure you're in an area with good GPS signal.

Navigation not showing correct route

The app uses real-time traffic data. If the route seems incorrect, you can use your preferred navigation app by tapping the address to open in Maps.
