Privacy Policy for Driver Partners

1. Introduction

Smart Move Driver ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our driver partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: By using Smart Move Driver, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name and contact information (phone number, email)
  • Profile photo and identification documents
  • Driver's license and vehicle registration
  • Bank account details for earnings withdrawal
  • Emergency contact information

Location Information

  • Real-time GPS location during active deliveries
  • Background location for job matching and tracking
  • Location history for trip records and earnings calculation

Vehicle Information

  • Vehicle type, make, model, and year
  • License plate number
  • Vehicle photos and documentation

Usage and Performance Data

  • Job acceptance and completion rates
  • Delivery times and routes taken
  • Customer ratings and feedback
  • App usage patterns and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify your identity and eligibility to drive
  • Match you with delivery jobs in your area
  • Provide real-time navigation and delivery tracking
  • Process your earnings and withdrawals
  • Calculate and display your performance metrics
  • Communicate job offers and important updates
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • Ensure safety and security for all users
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and develop new features

4. Location Data Usage

Background Location: Smart Move Driver requires background location access to:
  • Show your availability to customers
  • Match you with nearby delivery jobs
  • Provide accurate ETAs to customers
  • Track deliveries for safety and verification

You can disable location access in your device settings, but this will prevent you from receiving and completing jobs.

5. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Customers: Your first name, vehicle type, photo, and real-time location during active deliveries
  • Payment Processors: Stripe for earnings processing and withdrawals
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and communication services
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Business Partners: Aggregated, anonymized data for analytics
We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Firebase App Check and App Attest for app verification
  • Secure authentication with phone verification
  • Regular security audits and updates

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (processed within 30 days)
  • Location: Manage location permissions in device settings
  • Notifications: Control push notification preferences
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional communications

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active + 90 days after deletion
  • Trip Records: 3 years for dispute resolution
  • Financial Records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Location History: 90 days for active drivers

9. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • Firebase - Authentication and data storage
  • Mapbox - Maps and navigation
  • OneMap - Singapore mapping services

10. Children's Privacy

Smart Move Driver is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

11. Regional Privacy Rights

Singapore (PDPA)

Singapore residents have rights under the Personal Data Protection Act. Contact us at the email below to exercise your rights.

European Union (GDPR)

EU residents have additional rights including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights:
